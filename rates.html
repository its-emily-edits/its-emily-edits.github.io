03 — Process

How it works.

01

Brief & quote

You send a brief, I'll respond with a custom quote based on scope, exclusivity, and timeline.

02

Deposit & logistics

50% upfront, balance due on delivery. You ship product or provide direction; I handle rest.

03

Shoot & edit

I'll film, edit, and deliver within the agreed turnaround. Typically 10–14 days from product arrival.

04

Revisions & launch

Deliver draft, you request changes (up to 2 rounds included). Then you post, promote, or archive.

04 — Fine print

Terms & notes.

Pricing. All prices in USD unless noted. CAD quotes available on request — I work with both Canadian and US brands.

Usage rights. Base packages include organic use on the brand's owned channels in perpetuity. Anything else — paid ads, third-party placements, my personal channels — is an add-on.

Exclusivity. Without the exclusivity add-on, I retain the right to make content for non-direct-competitor brands at any time.

Revisions. Two rounds of revisions per video are included. Additional revisions are $50/round. Major creative pivots after approval may trigger a re-quote.

Turnaround. Standard turnaround starts when product is in-hand. Add 7–10 days buffer for shipping to Vancouver.

Disclosure. I follow FTC and Canadian Competition Bureau disclosure rules on any content where I'm tagged or where the brand is identifiable. Brands cannot ask me to omit disclosure.

Cancellation. Deposits are non-refundable once shooting has begun. Pre-shoot cancellations: 50% of deposit refunded.

Volume / agency rates. Talk to me. Repeat clients and agencies booking 5+ videos/month get retainer pricing.

Indicative pricing. Numbers above are my standard rates for typical UGC briefs. Final quotes account for scope, complexity, and ongoing relationship — feel free to ask.
